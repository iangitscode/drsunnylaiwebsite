About Dr. Sunny Lai
Dr. Lai has passionately serviced patients for their podiatric needs in Burnaby for the past 25 years. He obtained his undergraduate degree from the University of Western Ontario. He subsequently earned his medical degree from Rosalind Franklin University in Illinois. His further completed a residency specific in Podiatric Surgery at Vancouver General Hospital.
His approach to medicine is conservative. Although highly trained as a foot and ankle surgeon, he will exhaust all conservative care before recommending a surgical option.
Dr. Sunny Lai is most passionate about the sports medicine aspect of his practice: helping athletes young and old, novice and professional, to be their best. In his spare time, he enjoys photography, learning new languages, working out, hiking with his family and dog, open water swims, kayaking, camping and snowboarding.
Dr Lai is proud to be a member of:
  • College of Physician and Surgeons of BC
  • American College of Foot and Ankle Surgeons
  • BC Podiatric Medical Association
  • Canadian Podiatric Medical Association
About Dr. Midy Liou
We welcome our new associate Dr. Liou to the office. Dr. Liou is a well trained and experienced foot and ankle surgeon who treats a variety of lower limb problems including sports injuries, flat and high arched feet, bunion and hammertoes, warts, fungal toenails, and diabetic foot care.
Dr. Liou grew up in Vancouver and attended the University of British Columbia with a Bachelor of Science in Biology. He went on to earn his Doctorate of Podiatric Medicine from Barry University School of Podiatric Medicine in Miami, Florida. While in school, Dr. Liou volunteered at events to raise awareness and treated those with diabetic foot complications. Upon graduation, he completed a comprehensive three-year surgical residency in Foot and Ankle Reconstructive Surgery at Montefiore Medical Center and serving as Chief Resident in his third year. During his residency, Dr. Liou received extensive training and worked with the diverse population of New York City which helped him develop the skills and knowledge to treat a wide variety of foot and ankle problems. He has special interest in Minimally Invasive Surgical correction of bunion deformities, in which he has completed clinical research.
